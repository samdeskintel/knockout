Discovery questions — AE talk track

Let the prospect describe their own pain before you introduce samdesk.

On alert fatigue

Ask
"When a major incident unfolds — say, a protest near one of your facilities — how does that typically come through your platform? One notification or multiple?"
Ask
"How do your analysts manage the volume on a high-activity day? Is there a triage process before alerts are acted on?"

On manual triage burden

Ask
"After an alert comes in, what does it take to understand whether it's relevant to your assets and people?"
Ask
"How long does it typically take from first alert to having a situation report ready to brief up?"

On tool trust & partnership

Ask
"How configurable is your current platform — can your team adjust alert streams without involving the vendor?"
Ask
"How has the partnership felt over the last 12–18 months? Support, responsiveness, roadmap direction?"
Renewal landmine
"When did you last go through a renewal? What was that experience like?"
