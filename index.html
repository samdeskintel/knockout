AWARE framework — diagnostic tool for BDRs & AEs

Use this as a diagnostic conversation — not a product pitch. Let the prospect self-identify where they're stuck. Each stage is a place where AI compresses time and where most teams have room to mature.

A
Anticipate
Foresight before the incident — daily intel digest, historical search, pre-travel briefs
"Do we have foresight — or are we always catching up?"
W
Watch
Detection with context already attached — not just a raw signal. AI validates relevance and accuracy.
"Does detection come with context — or just an alert?"
A
Analyze
Where the 6 hours live. Instant org impact mapping, threshold applied, pre-assembled brief delivered.
"How long from alert to trusted decision — honestly?"
R
Resolve
AI-suggested response plans. Routine actions automated. Complex responses guided — not heroic.
"Does response depend on who's on shift?"
E
Enable
Full audit trail. Time-to-decision tracked. Board-ready reporting. Security earns investment.
"Can we prove we're improving — or just telling stories?"

If your CFO asked "is security improving?" — what would you show them?
Stories and anecdotes Some data, not systematic A dashboard with trends

Reading the answers

Mostly red — the operation is costing significantly more than it needs to, and leadership doesn't know it. Lead with the 96-hour cost calculation.

Mostly amber — they've started but the model hasn't changed. They're patching, not rebuilding. Lead with AWARE Watch + Analyze.

Mostly green — rare. Confirm what's working and find the one stage where they're still losing time.
